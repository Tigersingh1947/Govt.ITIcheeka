ITI Guhla-Cheeka is one of the most prestigious and renowned Industrial Training Institute of Haryana.
The aim of the institute is to provide Technically skilled manpower to industries by providing training in a safe 'hands on' workshop style environment.
The endeavour is aslo to reduce unemployment among educated youth by equipping them for suitable industrial employment- which form a amcro-perspective is an vital step in the direction of utilizing nation's demographic dividend.
It's qualified trainers who have up-to-date knowledge and experience of industry are dedicated to help mentees, realize their complete potential who can adjust to changing demands of industry as well as be future enterpreneurs.

⁂ Who we are exactly?

Government Industrial Training Institute Guhla at cheeka is a Haryana state Govt. Institution to provide technical training to youth for the purpose of employment in industry as skilled or semi-skilled workers running under Depatment OF Industrial Training Haryana panchkula.
It was established in 18 Oct. 2001.
The Department will continue its efforts toward the capacity, expansion and quality improvement in the field of Industrial Training with a view to achieve excellence in the training.
Industrial Training Institute was commenced by department of Industrial Training & Vocational Education , Haryana.
To enrich and empower all individuals via improved Employable Skills/ Knowledge in nationally and internationally recognozed qualifications to gain access to decent employment.
The motive of this institute is to technically trainn the youth with full facilities provided by Haryana Government inshape of good infrastructure and technically expert instructor under the supervision of qualified& experienced Group Instructor in order to prepare technically sound persona/ worker to enhance the Industrialproduction as well as to raise the productivity of the state/ Region.
