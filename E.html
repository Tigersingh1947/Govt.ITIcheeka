# ELECTRICIAN


* Trades Duration: Two Year
* Minimum Qualification Eligibility: * 10th Passed under 10+2 System with Science & Maths
* Trade Type: Engineering

* ITI trade Electrician is powered by NCVT. ITI trade Electrician is a job oriented trade ITI trade Electrician is suitable for government job and private job. This ITI trade Electrician is very powerful for self-empowerment. This ITI trade Electrician is perfectly design to fulfill industrial requirement of Indian Industries as well as International industries.


  1. * Electricians have a wide scope of Employability ranging from self-employment, contractual employment to Industrial jobs.

  2. * After successful completion of this course, Electricians can aspire to become Electrical Contractors by acquiring the ‘B” licence from the Electrical Licence Board.

  3. * They can set up their own Rewinding and servicing of Domestic Equipment shop.

  4. * Job opportunities are wide open in Defence, Railways, Transport, Ship Building, Electricity Board, various Industries etc

  5. * They can also go for further higher studies after successful completion of course.

  6. * Students who have completed this course have found employment in the following areas:

  7. * Service/Maintenance Technician for domestic appliances in Reputed Companies

  8. * Winder of Electrical Motors in winding shop

  9. * Contractor for domestic wiring and industrial wiring

  10. * Armature winder of Electrical fans and motors

  11. * Electrical appliance repair in electrical shops

  12. * Indian Railway (Asst. Driver, Tech. Gr. III, Appr. Technician)

  13. * Local Electricity Board

  14. * Assembler of Electrical control Gears

  15. * Telephone Department

  16. * Installation and Testing division of Auditorium and Cinema Hall

  17. * Factories

  18. * As Instructor in Govt./Private ITI/ITC

  19. * Merchant Navy

  20. * Indian Air Force

  21. * Self-Employment in Service Centre
